About

Research interests

My work combines computational and experimental approaches to understand plant traits, gene regulation, and crop improvement. I am particularly interested in genome and transcriptome analysis, public RNA-seq data reuse, and functional genomics in pearl millet.

Plant genomics

Trait discovery, comparative genomics, and breeding-oriented interpretation of sequence variation.

Transcriptomics

RNA-seq integration, large-scale reanalysis, and expression resource development across species.

Molecular biology

Gene-level functional analysis and biological validation of computational hypotheses.

Plant-microbe interactions

Orchid mycorrhiza, viral communities, and symbiosis-related molecular mechanisms.
